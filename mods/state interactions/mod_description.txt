[u][b] What is this mod? [/b][/u]
This mod adds a new menu when clicking on states - State Interactions! You'll find buttons for coring, uncoring, moving capital, requesting/removing resource rights and border wars. A perfect addition for RP games!

Interested in joining community RP games? Head on over to the Discardian Discord server: https://discord.gg/k7YHZ3EVEW

Coring/uncoring is based upon the Coring Button: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=1913968458

If you want to use anything that was added by this mod, get in touch through the discord link above. Thanks!
