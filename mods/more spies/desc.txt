[h1][u]HOI IV v1.11 updated[/u][/h1]
[img]https://i.imgur.com/XMixxyu.jpg[/img]
– maximum operative slots increased to 500; 
– 2 slots of the operatives in the establishment of the Agency; 
– the number of agents depends on the level of industry. There is a possibility to configure : for every 5/10/15/20/30/50 factories, 1 operative slot is given;
– "Illusive Gentleman" gives you 2 operative slots;
– added setting the maximum number of operatives from industry size.
P.S. This mod is part of our larger project - [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2197593000]New Ways[/url].
